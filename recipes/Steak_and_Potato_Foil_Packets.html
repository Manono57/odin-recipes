Steak and Potato Foil Packets

Description

These steak and potato foil packets are a fun dish that the whole family
will love and an easy clean up. The combination of steak, potatoes,
grilled onions, and bell peppers, seasoned to perfection

Ingredients

  • 1 pound small Yukon Gold potatoes, halved
  • 1 1/2 pounds sirloin steak, cut into bite-sized strips
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons butter, or as needed
  • 1teaspoon streak seasoning, or needed
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped, or more to taste
