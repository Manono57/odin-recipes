Rice

Description

Everyone should know how to cook rice. Most cuisines of the world are cooking rice in one way or another
We're also drinking our fair share of rice — in sake, horchata, rice milk, and even beer.
All told, we humans get more than 20 percent of our calories from this small but mighty grain.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Rice
  • 2 cups of water

Steps

  1. wash away excess starch
  2. Gently swish the rice with your fingers and rub lightly.
  3. Pour out the starchy water, Repeat one or two times, depending on how starchy the rice is.
  4. After pouring out the water for the last time, add clean water to the pot (add 1½ to 2 cups of water for 1 cup rice).
  5. Cover the pot and bring to a boil, then turn down the heat to its lowest setting. Let the rice gently simmer for 18 to 20 minutes.
  6. Remove the pot from the heat and let the rice continue to steam in the covered pot for five minutes
